* **Mean Free Path (MFP) Optimization:** Operating pressure is modulated to ensure that the MFP of electrons is optimized for collision-induced ionization rather than recombination against the Mu-metal-shielded walls.

* **The Townsend Discharge:** The high-voltage potential from the **Granite Transduction (Phase II)** initiates a localized electron avalanche, creating the initial conductive path.

* **Plasma Column Density:** The resulting pinch forms a high-density "plasma filament" capable of carrying the full inductive load of the singularity loop.

* **Harmonic Initiation:** The initiation pulse is synchronized with the resonant frequency of the stainless steel chamber, utilizing the "Still Point" to stabilize the plasma column during the peak compression phase. 

* **Thermal Modulation:** Active monitoring of the Bremsstrahlung radiation profile to ensure the plasma remains in a state of luminous equilibrium, preventing premature thermal quench.
